About

Jen Wright Photography, LLC provides both artistic and candid wedding photographs to couples in Virginia, Washington DC and Maryland. While she loves taking posed photographs in front of gorgeous backgrounds, she believes capturing candid emotions and experiences is equally important. Don’t worry if you’re not sure how to pose, Jen will help you relax so you can enjoy your big day. The company opened in 2014 and became a member of the Professional Photographers of America from 2014-2016.
